Sportclubby

See how we built a Sports Facility Booking System for athletes, enthusiasts, professionals, and federations

BRIEFDISCOVEROUTCOMESTECH

Overview

SportClubby makes it easier for individuals to locate/discover any sports facility and book courses, classes and fields right from the app. It is a holistic ecosystem through which people can search, promote and practice any kind of sport.

As a social management system for sports centres, SportClubby bridges the gap between sportsmen and clubs. People can join communities, make their own social circles and even challenge friends to ultimately hone their skills.

The Back-end for Clubs and Sports Federations

Clubs have a comprehensive view about how many followers they have, can create groups and send messages. Moreover, the back-end allows administrators to give approvals to followers to use a clubs facilities. Administrators can even create events and start promoting these as required. Detailed statistics are also available like user invites, challenges, participation, etc.

The biggest challenge was to create a highly customizable calendar that can manage camps, indoor activities (gyms) and outdoor activities (outdoor events): from tennis courts to yoga and diving lessons to sailing courses. Users book lessons or events, invite friends and share results on social networks, increasing visibility of both sports facilities and activities.
