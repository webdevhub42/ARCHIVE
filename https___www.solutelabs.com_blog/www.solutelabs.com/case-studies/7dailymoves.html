7 Daily Moves

Read how we developed a behaviour-changing exercise app that motivates people to adopt a healthier lifestyle

BRIEFCONTRIBUTIONIMPACTTECH

Overview

7DailyMoves is a mobile app that works as a personal trainer for daily workout. As an exercise app, it allows people to try out different exercises, keep track of them and motivates them to stay fit. Here’s how SoluteLabs helped 7DailyMoves realise its vision as a fitness product/start-up.

The story behind

Ms Sonam Mehra, the co-founder at 7 Daily Moves, is a fitness coach who has been working with individuals with health issues, teenagers, individuals who hate exercising, etc. for over ten years. She found out, most people hit the gym and spend hours doing ineffective workouts. Over the course of time, gymming equals socializing or doing what they are comfortable with. To keep them bound to daily workouts, something that could give instruction, variety, and goals, and could be used even while travelling, was needed. This also meant building something rewarding enough, specifically for people who don’t like to exercise.

Important areas that the app caters to

Real-Life Animations.
Guided Instructions.
Engaging Workouts.
Prevent Skipping Workouts.
Cost Savings.

People often feel a disconnect with and feel that bodies of people shown in exercising videos are simply impossible to achieve.

It doesn’t matter if you’re new to exercising apps or not, this app gives step-by-step audio instructions for working out.

Repetitive workouts will eventually bore people out even if they manage to focus on different muscle areas, fix this with 7dailymoves.

The app stores the progress of the workouts from where it was left as well as lets you create new workouts to encourage you.

7dailymoves is priced very affordably for a one-time fee for all future upgrades. This helps the user get a predictable pricing.

How SoluteLabs Helped

Brainstorming

Our team was involved right from the brainstorming to getting the app live on Google Play Store and iTunes. Since there were already many fitness mobile apps out there, the biggest challenge was to make sure the app was engaging and clearly stood out.

Gamification

To boost early adoption, one of the strategies we thought
was gamification.

This would keep the users engaged and ultimately lead
to favourable word-of-mouth. This involved having an
appealing, light-hearted, animated character which
makes users feel they can do workouts too.

We also did some motion designs to ensure the app went
viral. We made sure that the workouts listed in the app
were protected from IP infringement.

App Features

As humans, we are designed to do seven moves, namely - Squat, Push, Pull, Lunge, Bend, Twist, & Walk/Run/Jump. Any workout must include these 7 moves without which it is incomplete. 7DailyMoves offers the following SEVEN KEY FEATURES in its app -

Audio-based guided instructions for each exercise

Custom timer to ensure one can work out according to their convenience.

Effective workouts designed by fitness experts.

100+ workouts with the option to create custom workouts

Animated, guided exercises

Support for multiple languages

Save favourite workouts
