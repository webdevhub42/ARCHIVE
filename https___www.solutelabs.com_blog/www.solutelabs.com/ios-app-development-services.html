Why should you choose iOS App Development?02

Find out the critical reasons why most CTOs invest in Native iOS App Development

First-class Citizen

Cross Platform SDKs rely on backers and the open source community to add native features. Working with Swift gives you an upper hand and a better developer experience.

Long Term Support

Historically, Cross Platform SDKs have been short lived and new ones come before one matures - such as React Native, Kotlin Multi Platform, and Flutter. Developing with Swift ensures that you won’t be left stranded.

Closest to Metal

Apps developed with the official language Swift can be optimized through hardware acceleration and other techniques. Native Apps are usually smaller in size and better in performance.

User Experience

Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines are tailored to provide the best user experience on their devices. Users are habituated to use apps built on these design patterns which become second nature to them.

iWatch Apps

So far, none of the cross platform SDKs provide any strong support for iWatch development. With the rising trend in smart wearables, iWatch Development can only happen with native Swift.

Universal Apps

While you're developing apps for the iPhone, you can easily create a version for the iPad with minor tweaks; and with Mac Catalyst, you will be able use your iPad Apps on the Mac natively.

iOS App Development FAQs

Developing an application using Apple’s Operating System (OS) is called iOS development. This app could be for iPhones, iPads, Apple Watch, Apple TV, HomePod, and more

Building a simple yet UX-rich iOS application needs expertise and experience. But, hiring a highly skilled in-house iOS developer or a team becomes costly. That’s the reason why startups and enterprises outsource their most complex iOS app development to the right agency. It’s like getting ‘more in less.’

The debate between native and cross-platform app development has been in existence for years. But, having worked with both native and cross-platform projects, we can clear your doubts here-

If you’re looking to build highly complex mobile apps with super-high security, you must go with native iOS app development. With native iOS app development, the developers can access the most powerful OS and device features. Here, the cost is quite high as you need to code for both iOS and Android separately.

You must go for cross-platform app development when you’re working on time-critical projects and go-to-market is of utmost priority, like startups. Cost is comparatively low because cross-platform app development frameworks allow developers to code a few portions once, and use them multiple times.

Discuss your project requirements with us in detail and we’ll give you an honest suggestion on which would work best for your project.

Apple is making amendments to prepare its operating system to build apps for the coming generations. The more functional Apple is making its devices, the more important its operating system would become. This giant is bringing AR, IoT, and other such technologies to its devices, which will enable developers to build apps supporting its devices. So, iOS is going nowhere. It’s here to stay and so is its brilliant app development.
